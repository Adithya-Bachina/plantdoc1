About PlantDoc

A student-powered mission to rescue plants with tech brilliance!

Our Journey

We’re a team of passionate students who came together in 2025 to tackle a real-world problem: plant diseases. Armed with AI, creativity, and a love for greenery, we built PlantDoc to help plant lovers everywhere diagnose and save their leafy friends. It all started with a wild idea in a college classroom—and a lot of coffee!

From late-night coding sessions to heated debates over UI colors, we’ve poured our hearts into this project. Fun fact: Our first demo crashed spectacularly, but we turned it into a learning moment—and a pretty decent app!
